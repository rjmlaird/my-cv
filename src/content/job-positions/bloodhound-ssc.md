- Supported STEM outreach activities for the Bloodhound SSC education programme, contributing to national initiatives designed to inspire interest in engineering, physics, and high-speed vehicle technology.
- Developed educational resources for the BLAST outreach platform, translating advanced engineering and scientific concepts into accessible learning materials for students.
- Delivered interactive workshops in schools and public engagement settings, including hands-on demonstrations such as K’Nex car build challenges and applied physics activities.
- Engaged students through immersive STEM experiences, including the “Desert Living” workshop series, linking environmental science and engineering problem-solving.
- Assisted in communicating the technical and scientific goals of the Bloodhound SSC land speed record project to diverse audiences.
- Supported outreach events and presentations aimed at increasing participation in STEM subjects and showcasing real-world applications of physics and engineering.
